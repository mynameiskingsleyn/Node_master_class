The public record and credit reports contained in this portal are consumer reporting agency products provided by LexisNexis Risk Solutions, Inc and may only be accessed in compliance with the Fair Credit Reporting Act, 15 U.S.C. 1681 et seq (the ‘FCRA’). When you obtain these reports, you are a “User” of “Consumer Reports” as those are defined under the FCRA and as such certify as follows:

  1. User will use Consumer Reports for Employment Purpose under the FCRA and such reports will not be used for any other purpose.
  2. User understands that the enterprise has made a clear and conspicuous written disclosure to the consumer, prior to obtaining the report, in a document that consists solely of the disclosure that a Consumer Report may be obtained.
  3. User understands that the enterprise has obtained prior written authorization from the consumer and such information shall be retained only to the extent permitted by law.
  4. The information obtained will not be used in violation of any Federal or state law, including any equal opportunity law or regulation.
  5. If User intends to take any adverse action based in whole or in part on the Consumer Report, a copy of the report and a summary of the consumer’s rights will be provided to the consumer prior to taking adverse action.

User acknowledges that the FCRA imposes criminal penalties – including a fine, up to two years in prison, or both – against anyone who knowingly and willfully obtains information on a consumer from a consumer reporting agency under false pretenses, and other penalties for anyone who obtains such consumer information without a permissible purpose.

Notice: The Insider Threat Portal provides access to additional public record data when certain criteria is met, or potential Insider Threat Program risk is discovered or reported.

This additional data, which is not regulated by the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA), is marked "Non-FCRA" and segregated from the FCRA data.

By clicking Agree and Continue, the user acknowledges that the Non-FCRA data will only be used for analysis, inquiry or investigation of a person’s potential non-compliance with Federal, state, or local laws and regulations or matters related to internal preexisting written FBI policy.
